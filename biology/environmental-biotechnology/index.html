Environmental & Marine Biotechnology

This closing subject of the biology curriculum covers biotechnology applied to environmental problems and the marine environment - treating pollution biologically, replacing chemical agricultural inputs with biological alternatives, producing fuel from biological waste, and the distinctive biology and biotechnological potential of marine organisms.
