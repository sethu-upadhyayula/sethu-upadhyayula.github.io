Developmental Biology

Developmental biology asks how a single fertilized egg gives rise to a complete, patterned, multicellular organism - a process involving the same genes and cells dividing (Cell Biology) and expressing genes (Molecular Biology) as elsewhere, but now unfolding in a coordinated, spatially organized sequence. This subject covers the core concepts of development, gamete formation and fertilization, and the specific process of building a body plan in both animals and plants.
