Animal & Medical Biotechnology

This subject covers biotechnology applied to animals and human medicine - culturing animal cells, producing transgenic and cloned animals, the stem cell and regenerative medicine technologies transforming disease modelling and therapy, and the devices and diagnostics that connect biotechnology directly to clinical practice. Vaccine technology itself is covered in Chapter 7 of Immunology.
