Biomolecules & Biochemistry

This subject covers the structure and chemistry of the molecules that make up living systems, and the reactions and pathways that build, break down, and interconvert them. It starts with the four major classes of biomolecules, moves through protein and nucleic acid structure, enzyme catalysis, and then into the core metabolic pathways - bioenergetics, carbohydrate and lipid metabolism, nitrogen metabolism, and photosynthesis/respiration - before closing with the laboratory techniques used to separate and characterize these molecules.
