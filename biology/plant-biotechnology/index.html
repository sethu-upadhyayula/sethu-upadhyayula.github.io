Plant Biotechnology & Breeding

This subject covers biotechnology applied specifically to plants - from tissue culture and genetic transformation, through classical and molecular plant breeding, to the economic importance and disease vulnerabilities of cultivated plants. Vector systems and genome editing tools themselves are covered in Recombinant DNA Technology; this subject focuses on their plant-specific applications.
