Cell Biology

Cell biology examines the cell as the basic unit of life - how it is enclosed and organized, how its internal compartments each carry out specialized jobs, how it divides, and how it eventually dies. This subject moves from the plasma membrane inward, through the major organelles and the cytoskeleton, before covering the cell cycle and programmed cell death.
