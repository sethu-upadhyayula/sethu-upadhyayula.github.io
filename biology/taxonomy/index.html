Diversity, Taxonomy & Phylogenetics

This subject surveys the diversity of life and the principles used to classify it - from the rules of biological nomenclature through the major groups of microbes, protists, fungi, plants, and animals. Where Structural & Computational Biology covers the algorithms used to build a phylogenetic tree, this subject focuses on what those trees, and classification more broadly, reveal about the shape of life's diversity.
