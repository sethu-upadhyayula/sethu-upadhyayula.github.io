Helpmates

In a helpmate, both sides cooperate: Black and White work together toward a single goal - White delivers checkmate on the final move. The puzzle is to find the only path by which both sides, playing in concert, can reach checkmate in the stipulated number of moves. Black moves first.

The seven chapters below cover the helpmate genre from the ground up: the h#n stipulation and how to read it, the twin and duplex devices that let a single diagram encode multiple problems, the backward-analysis technique used to solve them, a fully worked illustrative example, the classic thematic content (ideal mates, echoes, Zilahi, Umnov), and the historical development of the genre from Max Lange's 1854 invention to its position today as the most-composed problem genre in the world.
