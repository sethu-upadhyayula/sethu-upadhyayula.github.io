Chess

I have been playing chess for many years and represented my university chess team, including serving as its captain for a year.

This section contains material related to chess in its different forms, from regular over-the-board chess to variants and compositions.

Some of it is meant to be instructional, while some of it simply reflects positions and ideas that I have found interesting.

Regular Chess

Notes on openings, middlegames, endgames, strategy, tactics, tournament play, and practical improvement.

Chess Variants

Notes on non-standard forms of chess, variant rules, unusual positions, and the different ways chess can be extended.

Chess Compositions

Studies, directmates, helpmates, selfmates, retros, fairy chess, and other composed chess problems.
