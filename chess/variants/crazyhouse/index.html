Crazyhouse

Crazyhouse is chess where captured pieces switch sides and can be dropped back onto the board as your own piece. Material is never permanently lost — every capture feeds the opponent's arsenal. This single rule transforms chess into a faster, sharper, more chaotic game where attack is everything and defence is always temporary.

The four chapters below take you from the basic drop rule through to the strategic and tactical consequences that make Crazyhouse one of the most popular and explosive chess variants: how material evaluation changes when nothing is ever truly lost, the spectacular drop-based tactics that decide most games, and the opening and practical principles that separate strong Crazyhouse players from the rest.
