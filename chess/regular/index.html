Regular Chess

Notes on over-the-board chess organised by phase and theme, from the rules and fundamental principles through to advanced strategy and endgame technique.

Basics of Chess

Piece movements, rules of the game, special moves (castling, en passant, promotion), and the fundamental principles of play.

Opening Theory

Opening principles, key systems for White and Black, pawn structures, and how to navigate the transition to the middlegame.

Middlegame Tactics

Forks, pins, skewers, discovered attacks, deflections, decoys, sacrifices, and combinations built from these motifs.

Middlegame Strategy

Pawn structure, outposts, weak squares, piece activity, rook placement, prophylaxis, imbalances, and long-term planning.

Theoretical Endgames

King and pawn endings, rook endings, minor piece endings, queen endings, Zugzwang, and essential theoretical positions.

Endgame Strategy

General endgame principles, evaluation, zugzwang and the opposition, and how to convert advantages or hold difficult defences.

Master Games

Annotated games by grandmasters, illustrating opening ideas, strategic plans, tactical combinations, and endgame technique in practice.

Chess History

From chaturanga and shatranj to the Romantic era, the first World Champions, the Soviet school, and the rise of chess engines.
