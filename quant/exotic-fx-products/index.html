Exotic FX Products

This section covers exotic FX derivatives that go beyond vanilla forwards and options: barrier options and target redemption forwards, whose payoffs depend on the path taken by the spot rate; accumulators and decumulators, structured strips of forwards favoured by corporates for FX hedging; multi-currency basket, quanto, and best-of/worst-of options, whose value depends on the joint behaviour of multiple exchange rates; and the risk management challenges — pin risk, discrete monitoring, and smile dynamics — that arise when running an exotic FX book.
