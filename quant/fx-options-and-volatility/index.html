FX Options and Volatility

This section covers the pricing of FX options under the Garman-Kohlhagen model (the FX analogue of Black-Scholes), the delta and hedging conventions specific to FX options markets, and the construction of the FX volatility surface from risk reversals and butterflies. It then turns to surface construction techniques, common option trading strategies, and the risk management of a vega/vanna/volga book across correlated currency pairs.
