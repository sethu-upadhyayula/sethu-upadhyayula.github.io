Concert Practice

This closing subject brings together everything covered elsewhere in Music - rāga (The Rāga System), tāla (The Tāla System), forms (Musical Forms & Compositions), and improvisation (Improvisation & Ornamentation) - into the standard live performance format they all serve: the modern Carnatic concert, or kutcheri. It covers how a concert is structured and programmed, how the ensemble's different roles interact, and the conventions performers and audiences alike are expected to know.
