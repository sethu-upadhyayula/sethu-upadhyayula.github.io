Math Modelling

Mathematical modelling uses the tools of calculus, analysis, and numerical methods to describe and solve problems arising in science and engineering. The five sections below cover optimization, ordinary and partial differential equations, finite element methods, and numerical analysis.

Optimization Methods

Linear and nonlinear programming, convexity, and methods for finding optimal solutions.

ODEs

Ordinary differential equations, existence and uniqueness, and methods for solving and analysing them.

PDEs

Partial differential equations, boundary value problems, and classical solution techniques.

FEM

Finite element methods for the numerical solution of differential equations.

Numerical Analysis

Numerical methods for solving mathematical problems, error analysis, and computational techniques.
