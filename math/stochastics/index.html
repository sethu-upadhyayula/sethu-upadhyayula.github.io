Stochastics

Stochastics is the study of randomness and its applications. The five sections below cover probability theory, statistical methods, stochastic processes, stochastic differential equations, and financial mathematics.

Probability Theory

Probability spaces, random variables, distributions, and limit theorems.

Statistical Methods

Estimation, hypothesis testing, regression, and statistical inference.

Stochastic Processes

Markov chains, martingales, Brownian motion, and other random processes evolving over time.

SDEs

Stochastic differential equations, Ito calculus, and their applications.

Financial Mathematics

Mathematical models for pricing, hedging, and risk management in financial markets.
