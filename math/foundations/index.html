Foundations

The five sections below cover the foundational material that underlies the rest of mathematics: formal logic and proof, the language of sets, calculus, linear algebra, and geometry. These topics form the common starting point for the more advanced material elsewhere in this section.

Mathematical Logic

Propositional and predicate logic, proof techniques, formal systems, and the foundations of mathematical reasoning.

Set Theory

Sets, relations, functions, cardinality, and the axiomatic foundations underlying the rest of mathematics.

Calculus

Limits, continuity, differentiation, integration, sequences and series, and their applications.

Linear Algebra

Vector spaces, matrices, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, and linear transformations.

Geometry

Euclidean and non-Euclidean geometry, coordinate methods, and the study of shapes, space, and transformations.
